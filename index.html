I am a graphic designer from a small dirt town in central Utah. Currently Associate Director of Design and Publishing at Carnegie Museum of Art. I have worked on projects with Other Means, Burnaway, Guggenheim New York, Meta Open Arts, and Tina Kim Gallery. Interested in books, animals, iconography, post cards, and gas stations.

For a PDF portoflio please email me.
