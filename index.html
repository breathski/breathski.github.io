I am a graphic designer from a dirt town in central Utah. Recent clients include Burnaway, The Carnegie Museum of Art, The Solomon R. Guggenheim Museum, Meta Open Arts and Tina Kim Gallery. I am interested in books, animals, iconography, post cards, and gas stations. Currently available for freelance.

For a PDF portoflio please email me.
